Ada Lovelace

Who is she?

Ada Lovelace was one of the first computer programmers in the world. She worked alongside Charles Babbage, another popular figure in the world of computer science. She is a figure for all women programmers, and programmers in general.


Quick Biography

Ada was born on December 10th, 1815. Her parents had legally separated when she was only two months old, and she never really got to know her father. She was educated privately, and was married to a man named William King, and became the Countess of Lovelace. She got to meet Charles Babbage, a man that she seemed interested in researching, and got to work alongside him for an analytical machine he was working on. She helped with the programming of the analytical engine, and her efforts are remembered to this day.
